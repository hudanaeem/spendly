Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and do not have independent access to your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
